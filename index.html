About Cricket:

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 11 players. It is played on an oval field with a rectangular pitch in the middle. The goal of the game is to score more runs than the other team.

How it's played:


  • The pitch is a rectangular area in the middle of the field, with a wicket at each end. A bowler bowls the ball towards the striker's wicket.


  • The striker tries to hit the ball with their bat to defend their wicket.
    If the striker hits the ball, they can run to the other wicket and score a point.

  • The bowling team tries to get the batter out by hitting the wicket with the ball, or by catching the ball before it reaches the ground.

  • The batting team continues to take turns batting and bowling until they make 10 outs or run out of overs.
